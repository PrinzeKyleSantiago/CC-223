What is Genshin Impact?

Genshin Impact is a free-to-play open-world action RPG developed and published by HoYoverse (formerly miHoYo). Released on September 28, 2020, the game is available on PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, iOS, and Android, with a planned release on Nintendo Switch in the future. The game takes place in the fantasy world of Teyvat, a vast and visually stunning open world divided into different regions, each inspired by real-world cultures and mythology. Players take on the role of the Traveler, who is searching for their lost sibling while uncovering the mysteries of the world.


Lore

Teyvat, a vast and mystical world divided into seven nations, each ruled by an Archon (god) who grants their people elemental powers. The story follows the Traveler, a mysterious being from another world, who is searching for their lost sibling while uncovering the secrets of Teyvat.
